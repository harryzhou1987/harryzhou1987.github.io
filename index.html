Embracing Change: Introducing Harry Zhou

Posted on 2023-10-20 In Engagement Disqus:

Hey there, amazing readers and friends!

I hope you are doing well as I write this. I have some exciting news to share
with you today: I’m starting the process of rebranding my Medium channel.

For those who’ve been following me under “Harry@StartQuick tech”, you might be
wondering “Why the change?”.

Well, I’ve been doing some soul-searching, and it’s time to bring more of my
authentic self to this platform.

Introducing Harry Zhou

From now on, I’m going by my real name, Harry Zhou. You
should notice the new username to match this change. This transition is more
than just a name; it’s a reflection of my commitment to authenticity and
building a more personal connection with you.

Why the Rebrand?

The decision to rebrand isn’t something I took lightly. I
believe that personal branding allows for a deeper and more meaningful
connection with my readers. It also gives me the freedom to explore a wider
range of topics and share my true passions and experiences with you.

What to Expect

So, what can you expect from Harry Zhou on Medium?

Well, it’s all about insightful content, genuine discussions, and a journey of
growth and exploration. I’ll be sharing articles on a diverse range of topics
that interest me, from technology to personal development and lifestyle.

Get Involved

I value your thoughts and opinions. If you have any questions,
suggestions, or simply want to say hello, feel free to leave a comment or reach
out.

Your feedback is invaluable in shaping this new chapter.

I will have my own tech blog website and newsletters soon. I will let you know
immediately once it is ready.

Thank you for being a part of this journey. I’m excited about the road ahead,
and I can’t wait to explore it with all of you.

Stay tuned for more engaging and thought-provoking content coming your way!

With gratitude,

Harry Zhou
